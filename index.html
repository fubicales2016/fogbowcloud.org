federation, opportunism and greenness in private infrastructure-as-a-service clouds through the barter of wares

Federation

Aggregate resources from multiple and heterogeneous infrastructure-as-a-service clouds.

federation@github

Opportunism

Deploy an infrastructure-as-a-service private cloud on top of shared commodity personal computers in an opportunistic fashion.

opportunism@github

Greenness

Save energy by consolidating applications in active hosts and by shutting down the idle ones.

greeness@github

Barter of Wares

Offer idle resources to other federation members when local demand is low, to get extra resources later from these members when local demand is higher than you can handle.

bartering@github
